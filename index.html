About Us

This website was created to work with a Myo armband. Moving up or down would change the note played, and making a fist would add a flat accidental. Holding a hand spread out for four seconds resets the current position, and rolling a hand either left or right allows the user to change his or her position on the piano before playing another note.

Yitz Deng and Rohan Sikdar, the creators of this site, are freshmen at University of California, Berkeley. Rohan studies Computer Science and Music, and Yitz studies Computer Science, Applied Mathematics, and Statistics.
